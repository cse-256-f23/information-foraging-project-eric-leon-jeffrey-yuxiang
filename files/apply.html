Application Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Application Fee

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one monthâ€™s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

Apply

You may fill out an Application for Admission online.
You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS.

Enrollment & Forms

Please fill out the online versions of the appropriate forms.

  • The Enrollment Form needs to be filled out every year that your child attends WUNS.
  • Once accepted into the school, fill out the Developmental History Form (only needs to be done once).
  • The two medical forms (below) are only available as PDFs and must also be filled out and returned.
